Brightside Medical Center

Brightside Family Medicine is a primary care practice located in the heart of the Akron, OH community. We specialize in personalized and high-quality medical care for the whole family. Our team of experienced doctors offers everything from preventive care to chronic disease management. We are dedicated to providing our patients with the most up-to-date treatment options and amenities to make your visit as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

Our most common services include:

  • Immunizations
  • Annual exams
  • Sports physicals
  • Much more
  • Certificates of Deposit
